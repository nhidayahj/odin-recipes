Aglio Olio Peperoncino

Photo by Yusuke Kawasaki from Flickr

Description

A classic Italian recipe for a simple yet flavourful pasta. A quick and simple meal for busy nights.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 (12 ounce) package spaghetti
  • 6 tbs extra-virgin olive oil or to taste
  • 1 chilli pepper, seeded and mince
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup of chopped fresh Italian parsley

Steps

  1. Bring large pot of salted water to a boil.
  2. Cook spaghetti in boiling water, stirring occasionally about 2 mins before cooking time on package.
  3. Drain and reserve 1 cup of pasta water.
  4. Heat olive oil in a large skillet
  5. Add chopped chilli and garlic; stir until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  6. Pour 1/4 cup of reserved pasta water and stir
  7. Add drained pasta to skillet and toss
  8. Mix in parsley and toss.
  9. Plate pasta with remaining olive oil, if desired. Serve immediately.
